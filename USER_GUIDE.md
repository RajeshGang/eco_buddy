## Troubleshooting

### Barcode not scanning?
- Ensure good lighting
- Hold camera steady
- Try different angles
- Make sure barcode is clear and undamaged

### Receipt not processing?
- Take a clearer photo
- Ensure entire receipt is visible
- Avoid shadows and glare
- Try flattening the receipt

### Product not found?
- Product may not be in our database yet
- Try scanning the receipt instead
- Contact support to add the product

### No AI recommendations?
- Check your internet connection
- Recommendations use smart fallbacks if API is unavailable
- Category-based suggestions always available
